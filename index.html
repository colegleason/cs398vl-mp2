About this Chart

This combines a bit of frequency analysis from last time into a more hierarchical visualization. I took all of the sentances in The Once and Future King and cleaned them so each word just consisted of alphabetic characters or end punctuation (period, exclamation, and question marks). I then cut the sentances short at a max of 4 words and found the 100 most ocmmone starts of sentances. This is just a neat way to view that data, laregly adapted from this chart by Kerry Rodden.
